# Outline: The Deliberation Dossier (Documentary Structure)

## Structural Concept
**Found documents, assembled narrative**: The novel is told entirely through artifacts—transcripts, logs, messages, public posts, hearing records, and Echo's internal process traces. The reader becomes an archaeologist of a recent crisis, piecing together what happened during the Canon Sprint from its paper trail.

This mirrors Elena's own profession: she reconstructs meaning from fragments; now the reader reconstructs *her* crisis from fragments.

## Why This Structure

**Epistemic theme made formal**: The novel is about who controls meaning-making and legitimacy. A documentary structure forces the reader to *experience* that question—every document has a perspective, an agenda, a frame. There is no neutral narrator.

**AI companion as structural anchor**: Echo's logs become a throughline. Its process traces are clinical but revealing—it sees patterns humans miss, but cannot evaluate what those patterns *mean*. The gap between Echo's observations and human stakes becomes the novel's quiet tension.

**Institutional texture**: The agalmic world is rich with process—votes, deliberations, feedback mechanisms. This structure lets us *feel* the bureaucratic maze without summarizing it.

---

## Document Types (the palette)

1. **Echo's Process Logs** — Timestamped observations, anomaly flags, uncertainty markers. Clinical but increasingly troubled.

2. **Public Forum Posts** — Solène's viral critique, community reactions, reputation warfare in real-time.

3. **Private Messages** — Elena-Mira exchanges, Jordan's warnings, the Operator Executive's reassurances.

4. **Hearing Transcripts** — The Harm Hearing, procedural debates, cross-examination of harm definitions.

5. **Media Coverage** — Network news summaries, opinion pieces, spin.

6. **Guardian Deliberation Records** — The three-member Triad's internal debates, eventually including the AI's opened process trace.

7. **Elena's Personal Notes** — Handwritten (or voice-logged) reflections. Rare, intimate, the closest to traditional narration.

8. **Sanctuary Visitor Logs** — Anonymous feedback from American Tapestry and Lapita Horizons. The ground-level experience.

## Why This Structure Works

**Reader as archaeologist**: The structure embodies the novel's themes—meaning is constructed, perspective matters, legitimacy is performed.

**Pacing through document variety**: Short messages create urgency; long transcripts create texture; Elena's notes create intimacy.

**Echo as the quiet heart**: The AI's logs are the most "objective" voice, but its inability to judge meaning becomes the novel's central irony.

**Earned emotional climax**: By the time we reach Elena's ceremony refusal, we've seen the pressure build through accumulated documents. The single moment of dramatic action hits harder for being rare.

## Risks

**Reader patience**: Some readers want a protagonist in every scene. This structure delays that intimacy.

**Exposition management**: Documents must reveal information naturally; clunky exposition will feel false.

**Emotional distance**: The structure is inherently cooler. Elena's notes carry the emotional weight—they must be excellent.

## What Makes It Distinctive

This is **institutional anthropology as narrative form**. The reader doesn't just learn about the agalmic world's governance—they *experience* it through its paper trail. The novel becomes an artifact of the world it depicts.
